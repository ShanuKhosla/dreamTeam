About The Event


Awaited by cricket and finance enthusiasts alike, presenting you all “DREAM TEAM 2021”, a premium event hosted by Dream Merchants perennially. Dream Team replicates the actual IPL auction. It showcases the meticulous planning behind every multi-crore bid for Virat Kohli, every composition that Dhoni makes, every team switch by Maxwell, etc. Participants act as delegates for IPL teams, attempting to meet the team's requirement to the best of their abilities.

This event comprises of 3 rounds.

Round 1(The elimination round) will be a quiz consisting of 30 multiple choice questions (Duration: 8 mins Platform: Kahoot). The questions will be based on cricket or IPL domains. The top 32 participants will advance onto the next round. Judging criteria for this round is accuracy and speed.

In Round 2, The 32 participants are segregated into four groups, each with eight people. The qualified participants will compete in round 2 and the top 2 participants from each slot will advance to the third round. Consequently, the eight finalists from round 2 will play in round 3. (It is implied that the rules for round 3 are same as round 2).

The event will be conducted on the Zoom platform. An auctioneer will share the player’s name and base price in the meeting. The highest ratings of the best 11 players will be used to determine the winners.



Rules

The Dream Team will consist of 2 rounds

  • In round 1, the 10 teams (consisting of 4-5 participants in each team) will participate in the dream team auctions held by DREAM MERCHANTS team.
  • The IPL team allotted will be chosen by a chit system where each team representative will pick a chit. IPL TEAM ALLOTTED ON CHIT IS FINAL ,NO CHANGES
  • The event will be conducted in . An auctioneer will announce the player’s name and the base price in the venue.
  • Winners will be declared based on sum total of the highest rated 11 players
  • Each team will be given a budget of ₹25k and the team will have to keep an account of their budget and the players they bought.
  • If the team exceeds the given budget, the team will be disqualified then and there.
  • The players' list (160 players) will be provided beforehand we will be rating each player randomly (with slight modifications based on the rating given by various cricket associations).
  • To place a bid, the teams are supposed to raise their hand only.
  • The auctioneer's judgment will be considered as final.

  • The bid will be incremented as follows
    Price Bracket Bid Increment
    200-500 20
    500-1000 50
    1000-5000 100
    5000-10000 200
    10000+ 500
  • The team must have atleast:
    1. 4 Bowlers
    2. 4 Batsmen
    3. 2 All Rounders
    4. 1 Wicket Keeper
    5. Your team (top 11 players) should have at least 4 overseas players
  • Slots will be allotted to the selected participants (10 participants per slot).
  • For each slot, the duration of the IPL auction will be approximately 3 hours
  • The auctioneer will call out the name of a player and the details will be displayed via the presentation.
  • After the presentation (details) has been displayed the participants can start bidding
  • While bidding, the participants are supposed to raise their hands and then place their bid.
  • Max. of 15 seconds will be given to the participants to bid.
  • After exactly 10 seconds, if no bid has been placed, the player will go unsold
  • You can buy more than 11 players, but only the best 11 players will be taken into consideration. Each participant can buy a maximum of 14 players.

Criteria of selection of winners from each slot:

  • After the auction ends, the sum of ratings of the top 11 players of each team will be considered. The team with the highest score will be declared the winner of that slot.

The Breaker Situation

  • The participant who has more budget left will be given preference
  • The participant who has the highest ratings of its batsman.
